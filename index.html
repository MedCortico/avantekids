About Avante Kids

Avante Kids is a premier multi-speciality pediatric clinic offering comprehensive healthcare services under one roof. Our team of experienced specialists is dedicated to providing exceptional care for children from newborns to adolescents.

Our vision is to deliver expert and passionate healthcare for every child, ensuring their healthy development and well-being.

Our Services

Immunization

Comprehensive vaccination services following international and national immunization schedules to protect your child from preventable diseases.

General Pediatrics

Regular check-ups, growth monitoring, and treatment for common childhood illnesses and conditions.

Neonatology

Specialized care for newborns, including premature babies and those with special health needs.

Pediatric Gastroenterology

Diagnosis and treatment of digestive, liver, and nutritional problems in children.

Child Development

Assessment and support for developmental milestones, behavioral issues, and learning difficulties.

Pediatric Surgery Consultation

Expert consultation for children requiring surgical interventions with referrals to specialized surgeons.
